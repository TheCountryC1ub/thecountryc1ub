Questions

Before you download.

What is Elite Golf Consulting?

Elite Golf Consulting is an iOS performance app for golfers who want to actually improve. It works as a performance tracker and a journal in your pocket — logging your rounds, stats, practice and mindset, then showing you exactly where you're losing strokes.

What does the app track?

Your round-by-round progress; the stats that move your handicap (fairways, greens, putts, up-and-downs, sand saves); your wedge score; your wins and milestones; and AI Intelligence Reports that analyse your game like a Tour caddie after every round.

What are the 9 pillars of golf?

The 9 Pillars are the core dimensions every great round is built on. Most golfers can't name even three of them. The app scores you across all nine and tells you what to fix first — the full framework is revealed inside the app.

Who is Elite Golf Consulting for?

Golfers of any level who want to stop guessing and start improving with real numbers — from weekend players trying to break a plateau to low-handicappers chasing the next tier.

Is there a community? Can I ask the pros questions?

Yes. Elite Golf Consulting is more than a tracker — it's a community. Connect with other golfers, share your progress and your setbacks, and support each other through the grind. You can also take your questions straight to elite and professional players inside the app.

Who created Elite Golf Consulting?

It was built by Cameron Tennant and a team who have competed at a high level — born from years chasing the game, once living out of a van in pursuit of The Masters. Built by pros, powered by AI.
