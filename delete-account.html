Delete your account.

Your account is yours. If you want it gone, here is exactly how — no hoops.

Option 1 · Fastest

Delete it inside the app

  1. Open the Elite Golf Consulting app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Choose Delete Account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your account and the data tied to it — your profile, rounds, practice logs, assessments, and community posts. It cannot be undone.

Option 2 · By email

Request deletion by email

No longer have the app installed? Email us from the address on your account and we'll take care of it. We confirm every request and complete deletion within 30 days.
